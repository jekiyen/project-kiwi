# Project Kiwi — Vision

## Product Vision

Project Kiwi is a personal AI migration copilot built for one person: Rizky. Its purpose is to reduce the manual effort, uncertainty, and cognitive load of migrating from Indonesia to New Zealand.

It starts with the hardest first step — finding the right job — and grows over time into a comprehensive assistant that guides the entire journey: from first job search, through visa application, to relocation and settlement.

Project Kiwi is not a product. It is a personal tool. Every decision is optimized for a single user's specific situation.

---

## Long-Term Goals

1. **Job Discovery** — Automatically surface the most relevant blue-collar job opportunities in New Zealand, ranked by fit and visa eligibility.

2. **Application Intelligence** — Assist with writing targeted cover letters, tailoring the resume per role, and submitting applications efficiently.

3. **Visa Awareness** — Maintain a clear view of which visa pathways are available, which employers are accredited, and what documents are needed at each stage.

4. **Migration Tracking** — Organize every task, document, and decision involved in the migration process in one place.

5. **Settlement Support** — Once a job is secured, assist with housing research, cost of living, and building a life in New Zealand.

---

## Success Metrics

### V1
- Discovers and ranks relevant jobs from all 6 target platforms within the first week of operation.
- Reduces daily job search time from hours to under 15 minutes.
- Surfaces at least 3–5 high-quality matches per week.
- Zero relevant jobs silently missed or incorrectly discarded.
- User trusts the system enough to rely on it as the primary job discovery tool.

### Long-Term
- User secures a job offer from a New Zealand employer.
- User successfully migrates to New Zealand.
- System correctly identifies and tracks all viable visa pathways throughout the journey.

---

## Non-Goals

- **Not a public product.** Project Kiwi will never be marketed, multi-tenanted, or sold.
- **Not a legal advisor.** The system provides information and context about visas, but does not give legal immigration advice.
- **Not a general job board.** It is purpose-built for one person's specific job targets and migration context.
- **V1 does not automate applications.** Automated submission comes only after the user trusts the quality of matches.
- **V1 does not modify the resume.** All AI suggestions require explicit user approval before any changes are applied.
